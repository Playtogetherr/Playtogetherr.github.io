ABOUT US

Play Together is a simulation adventure game by Haegin Co., Ltd. Launched in January 2021 on the iOS platform, so far the number of game downloads has reached the number of rows. The game is labeled 4+, suitable for almost all ages.When joining the game, you will be given your character options. The game simulates a miniature society when you can control the character to participate in fun activities, build houses, fish and socialize with your friends in the game. During a time when the Covid 19 service was still complicated, Play Together has done a good job of connecting and building a community of players to help it become the top 1 game in recent months in Vietnam. Game Play Together is available on both IOs and Android operating systems, you can link to your iCloud, CH Play or Facebook account, this will help you connect with your friends during the game.
